I've recently made the decision to go VIM … or at least try. The first time I've heard it mentioned was on CSS Wizardry. Being a fan of Harry Robert's recent talks I decided to download it and see what the hype is about.

Upon first opening it up I immediately felt the same way I did about SASS, overwhelmed and horrified that this might require use of the command line, which sure enough, it did.

The next several days should be interesting, but as I want to continue growing as an artist, for some strange reason, this just feels right. I'll continue to document my experience via this blog.
